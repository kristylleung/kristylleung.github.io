Introduction

Sojourn

We are a coffee shop in downtown San Luis Obispo with a mission to cultivate a culture of openness, authenticity, and compassion through quality brewed coffee. We hope you'll stop by and say hello.

Target audience

Our customers are coffee and tea drinkers with an interest in connecting with others. While our visitors tend to be young adventurers or working adults, we're eager to serve anyone who's interested in a cup of coffee, brewed with care.

We aim to serve our community with openness and compassion. While Sojourn at its core remains a coffee company, we also open our doors to creatives and musicians who'd like to share their work. We hope that our coffee can be a bridge for new connections in both personal and professional spheres.
